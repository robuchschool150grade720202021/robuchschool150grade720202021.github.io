2020-2021 - A memorial of our times together

The year 2020 began with several out of the ordinary events and at last the great pandemic

that had already before the story of 7th grade kept the children at home since March-April


At first schools re-opened in autumn however soon the schools were closed again and the

trials of online school began, the battle was tremendously difficult but despite this

everyone managed to survive, even those that sometimes weren't in online classes made it

out alive in fact. Alive for what?


Even though the "why" normally doesn't normally matter this is a great gateway towards opening

the doors of the classroom, a great reunion and all the experiences that ensued in the following month

culminating in a great end for the school year at last on Wednesday the 23rd of June 2021-










hint for future people: Courage is the magic that turns dreams into reality-
